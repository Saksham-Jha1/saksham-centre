About Saksham Computer Centre

Empowering learners through technology and trusted education.

Our Story

In today's fast-changing world, digital literacy is not just a skill. It is a necessity. From managing personal finances to building a career, almost every part of modern life requires a solid understanding of technology. Understanding this essential need, we are excited to introduce Saksham Computer Centre, your local hub for quality computer education.

'Saksham' means 'capable' or 'competent,' and that is exactly what we want to achieve for every student. We are more than just another computer institute; we are a center of excellence, supported by the proven teaching methods and strong curriculum of Drishti Computer Education, a name known for its quality and trust in the IT training field. If you want to close the gap between where you are and where you want to be in the digital world, Saksham Computer Centre is the perfect starting point.

Our Partnership

Choosing the right educational institution is the most important decision in your learning journey. At Saksham Computer Centre, we remove that uncertainty by operating as an authorized franchise of Drishti Computer Education. This partnership is both our strength and your advantage. It means you receive local, personalized attention and support while your education is backed by a national organization. This organization has years of experience, maintains consistent quality, and stays updated with industry trends.

Drishti Computer Education offers a carefully crafted, industry-relevant curriculum that is updated regularly to include the latest software, programming languages, and job market needs. When you receive a certificate from Saksham Computer Centre, you earn a credential that is recognized and respected widely. This quality assurance guarantees that your investment in your future is safe and worthwhile, ensuring that the skills you gain are marketable and useful in the real world. This structured approach forms the foundation for your digital skills.

Our Courses

Our course offerings at Saksham Computer Centre are designed to cater to a diverse audience, from absolute beginners to professionals seeking advanced specialization. We understand that a one-size-fits-all approach does not work in technology.

For those taking their first digital steps, our foundational courses cover essential areas like operating systems, Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), and internet literacy. These programs provide the fundamental confidence needed to navigate the modern workplace and educational environment.
